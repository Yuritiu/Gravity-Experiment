Gravity Experiment is a small prototype built around directional gravity and unusual movement.

You play as a tiny character trapped inside a transparent maze in a home office environment, using gravity shifts to reorient yourself and find a way out. Once you escape, you can move through the surrounding open space and experiment with the system further.





W / A / S / D - Shift your fall direction relative to the camera

Q - Fall downward and realign with the ground

E - Fall upward and realign with the ground

Right Mouse - Connect to object
